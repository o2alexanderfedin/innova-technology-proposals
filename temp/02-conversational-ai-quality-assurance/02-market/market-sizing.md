## Executive Summary

The conversational AI quality assurance and testing market represents a $1.4-2.8 billion opportunity within the broader $17-19 billion conversational AI market in 2025. Hupyy's mathematical validation platform targeting chatbot development agencies and enterprise AI teams addresses a critical gap in an industry where 27% of chatbot responses contain inaccuracies and hallucination rates range from 9-30% depending on domain complexity. The serviceable obtainable market (SOM) for Innova Technology represents $12-18 million in Year 1, scaling to $85-120 million by Year 3 as the platform captures market share in the rapidly growing chatbot QA segment (23.8% CAGR).
